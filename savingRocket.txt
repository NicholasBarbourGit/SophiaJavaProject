
Some incredible instinct kicks in a takes over. You're completely unsure and unfamiliar with 
the surrounding environment. In fact, you look outside and see the endless expanse of space.
It is truly beautiful and unlike you have ever witnessed or experienced before. You keep
running through the metal corridors, following signs with symbols you don't recognize. 

Eventually, you find your way to the hanger bay. You find a vessel that looks appropriately sized
for a single traveler. You quickly jump inside and are overwhelmed by the controls. Nothing in here
makes any sense. There is, however; a ring sitting atop the would be dashboard. It has a symbol
you do recognize. A full body and this ring sits atop the body's head. With no other viable options
you place the device on your head. 

Suddenly, you hear a voice, "Where would you like to go......"
